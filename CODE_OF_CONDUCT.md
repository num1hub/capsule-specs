# Code of Conduct

N1Hub is committed to a respectful, technically rigorous, and welcoming community.

## Expected behavior

- act with respect and professionalism
- critique ideas, code, and architecture without attacking people
- document disagreements with evidence instead of hostility
- protect private or sensitive information
- help keep the project legible for future contributors

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- doxxing or publication of private data
- abusive language, threats, or sustained disruption
- misrepresenting repository state, authorship, or validation results

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior threatens contributor safety or repository integrity. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.
